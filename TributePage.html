Nikola Tesla

One the greatest inventors to have ever lived

Here's a timeline of Nikola Tesla's life
  • 1859 - Born in the Smiljan, Austrian Empire, now Croatia.
  • 1870 - Contracts cholera, then hospitalized for a year, almost didn't make it.
  • 1875 - Enrolled in Polytechnic with military scholarship. Achieved highest grade possible in most courses.
  • 1880 - Takes up philosphy due to a lack in Greek and Czech literacy.
  • 1881 - Moves to Budapest to work for a telegraph company. Becomes chief electrical engineer, during his tenure, he introduced various operational upswings that improved the performance of the company.
  • 1882 - Finds another job in Paris with a company referred to as the Continental Edison Company. Relocates to the USA permanently.
  • 1885 - Meets with attorney Lemuel Serrel who helps with submission of various patents. Serrel introduced Tesla to various businessmen, including Benjamin Vail and Robert Lane. They agreed to help finance a new light production company under Tesla Name. Tesla worked to acquire the patents for the company including developing an enhanced DC motor. His solutions gained attention from the mass media who sought his solutions.
  • 1887 - Comes up with an induction motor that was operated by alternating current
  • 1890 - Tesla Begins His Work On Wireless Power Transmission
  • 1891 - Tesla Patents The Tesla Coil
  • 1943 - Dies of conorary complications in a hotel room in Newyork
